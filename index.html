escrow protection

No one loses. Ever.

Funds are locked the moment a trade opens and only move when both sides are satisfied. If something goes wrong, a moderator steps in.

01

Escrow lock

The seller's balance is frozen in benzuko escrow before the buyer sends a single unit of payment.

02

Pay & confirm

Buyer pays through the agreed method and marks it done. Real-time chat keeps proof in one place.

03

Seller releases

Seller verifies receipt and releases. Funds land instantly — most trades close in under four minutes.

04

Appeal & resolve

Disagree? Open an appeal. A trained moderator reviews the chat, evidence and audit log, then decides.

// open an escrow-backed payout

// funds locked until both sides confirm
